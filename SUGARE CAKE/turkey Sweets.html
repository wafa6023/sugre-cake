Turkish sweets

Turkish sweets have become famous, especially among Arabs, and won the admiration of Arabs, as they have become one of the sweets that we offer now on our occasions with coffee, and in this blog we mentioned the most prominent and best Turkish sweets that won the admiration of many people

Baklava:



Bring a medium-sized dish, heat the oven to a temperature of two hundred
degrees Celsius, then put the ghee and butter or oil in a deep saucepan and
melt the butter on a low heat. Put: rose water, pistachio and sugar in a deep dish, and stir the materials
together until they are mixed well, then leave the mixture aside.Cut the dough using a knife so that it is the same size as the plate without
separating the chips from each other, then put the plate on top of the dough
then put half the amount of chips in the plate, distribute the filling over it
put the rest of the chips on top of the pistachios, then cut the chips using
the knife as desired, and pour a mixture Butter and ghee on top.Put the dough into the oven for twenty-five to thirty minutes until it turns
golden, then take it out, distribute the syrup directly over it and leave
it until it becomes cold.














lokum:



Over medium heat, put sugar, lemon juice and 1 ½ cup of water, stirring slowly
until sugar dissolves. Leave the mixture to boil, brushing the
inside of the pot with a brush wet with water from time to time, so that the sugar does
not caramelize When the temperature of the mixture reaches 225 degrees, remove it from the heat
Dissolve the starch in the water, then put it on a medium heat
with the cream of tartar, stirring constantly until it boils and thickens Add the sugar mixture to the starch mixture, stir, and simmer after five hours
stirring every 10 minutes. Raise the mixture from the fire, and introduce
rose water and color, and mix well Pour the mixture into trays covered with nylon, or into silicone trays,and leave
overnight to cool. Remove the candy from the trays, then coat it
with powdered sugar, and cut it with an oiled knife so that it does
not stick to the knife into squares Dip each square in powdered
sugar to cover it on all sides.














Künefe:




Dissolve the starch with cold milk in a saucepan, then put it on the fire
while continuing to stir until the mixture becomes a little thick
Add the sweet cream and continue to stir for 3 minutes, then remove the mixture
from the heat and add the cream, then stir the mixture until the ingredients mix well
and leave the mixture aside to cool.Put the kunafa in a large bowl, add the melted ghee and mix well
Bring the oven tray and put half the amount of kunafa in it then add
the previously prepared cream and put the other half of the kunafa .Put the tray at a temperature of 200 degrees until golden.Remove the tray from the oven, then add the syrup to it, ready to serve.
Always served with Turkish coffee
















Trileçe:


Turn on the oven at 170 degrees Celsius, before preparing the trellica
In a bowl, add the eggs and beat with the vanilla, then add the sugar
until the mixture reaches a white color and becomes creamy
Then gradually add the amount of oil, whisking constantly, and gradually the milk
until you have finished the amount of milk.Then add the flour after mixing the baking powder, and beat on the lowest
speed with an electric beater.Pour the trilitha cake mixture into
a greased and floured mould Put it on the middle rack in the oven for 40 minutes
until the trellica cake is baked.a cup of milk A cup of cream.A spoon and a half of sugar
Vanilla spoon.Mix the ingredients together, and pour the trellica cake
with the milk and cream mixture Mix the ingredients of milk, sugar and powdered milk in
the blender, and add to them a cup of fine thread oil in the blender
and mix until the mixture thickens and you get the white chocolate sauce
and after it sticks, add the famous caramel sauce to it
